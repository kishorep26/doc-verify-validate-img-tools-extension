# Privacy Policy for Document Verify & Image Tools

**Last Updated**: December 19, 2024

## Overview

Document Verify & Image Tools is a Chrome extension that respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your data.**

Specifically:
- ❌ No personal information collected
- ❌ No document numbers stored
- ❌ No images uploaded to servers
- ❌ No usage analytics or tracking
- ❌ No cookies or identifiers
- ❌ No third-party services

## How the Extension Works

### Document Verification
- Aadhar and PAN validation happens **entirely in your browser**
- Numbers are processed using local JavaScript algorithms
- Nothing is sent to external servers
- No data is saved after you close the extension

### Image Processing
- All image operations (resize, crop, compress, convert) happen **locally**
- Images are processed using HTML5 Canvas API in your browser
- Processed images are downloaded directly to your computer
- Original and processed images are not stored by the extension

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests minimal permissions:

1. **storage**
   - Used to save your preferences (like last used settings)
   - Data stays on your device only
   - You can clear this anytime in Chrome settings

2. **downloads**
   - Required to download processed images to your computer
   - No data is uploaded anywhere

## Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit data, there's no data to secure on our end. All processing happens on your device.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to Privacy Policy
